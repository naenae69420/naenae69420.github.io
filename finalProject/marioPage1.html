Mario Kart Remastered

Game Rules and About

Mario Kart Remastered is my own take on Mario Kart, but as a web application! The goal is essentially to get to 500 points. By racing other opponents via your speed, one will have an advantage over the other based on that. However, the game also provides powerups and items, similar to that of the original game. Race against your opponents, and may RNG be on your side!
