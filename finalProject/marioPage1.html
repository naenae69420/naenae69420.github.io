Mario Kart Remastered

About

Mario Kart Remastered is my unique spin on the classic Mario Kart, reimagined as a web application! The objective is simple: reach 500 points by racing against opponents, where speed plays a key role in gaining an advantage. However, just like the original game, you can collect power-ups and items to turn the tide in your favor. Race against your rivals and may RNG be in your favor!

Game Rules

The game will consist of "laps," each requiring you to click a button until all players reach a score of 500. At the start, each player will be randomly assigned a "base speed," which will determine their advantage over others. With each lap, the player's base speed will be multiplied by 50, giving them a growing advantage. Every 5 laps, all players will receive a power-up, either a buff or a debuff, that can have a positive or negative effect on them. This ensures that everyone has a chance to win! You can learn more about these power-ups on the next page.

Evolution of Mario Kart

Mario Kart began as Super Mario Kart in 1992, released on the Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES). Created by Nintendo, the game introduced the concept of kart racing with beloved Super Mario characters, unique power-ups, and multiplayer action. It was a hit, blending fun racing with strategic items like shells and bananas. The series expanded with sequels, each introducing new features like 3D graphics (Mario Kart 64, 1996), motion controls (Mario Kart Wii, 2008), and anti-gravity racing (Mario Kart 8, 2014). Portable versions, such as Mario Kart DS (2005) and Mario Kart 7 (2011), brought the fun to handheld consoles.
