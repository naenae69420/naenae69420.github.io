Mario Kart Remastered

Game Rules and About

Mario Kart Remastered is my unique spin on the classic Mario Kart, reimagined as a web application! The objective is simple: reach 500 points by racing against opponents, where speed plays a key role in gaining an advantage. However, just like the original game, you can collect power-ups and items to turn the tide in your favor. Race against your rivals and may RNG be in your favor!
